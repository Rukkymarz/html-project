Indian Tikka Masala Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps to Prepare Tikka Masala:

  1. Marinate the Chicken: In a large bowl, mix the yogurt, lemon juice, cumin, coriander, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, minced garlic, and minced ginger. Add the chicken cubes, mix well, and let it marinate for at least 1 hour, preferably overnight in the refrigerator.
  2. Cook the Chicken: Preheat your grill or oven to medium-high heat. Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto skewers and grill or bake for about 10-15 minutes, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred. Set aside.
  3. Prepare the Masala Sauce: In a large pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until they are golden brown.
  4. Add the tomato puree, garam masala, and salt. Cook the mixture on medium heat for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add Cream: Lower the heat and slowly stir in the heavy cream. Simmer the sauce for another 5 minutes, stirring constantly to prevent it from curdling.
  6. Combine Chicken with Sauce: Add the grilled chicken pieces to the sauce and let it simmer for an additional 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  7. Garnish and Serve: Remove from heat, garnish with fresh cilantro, and serve hot with basmati rice or naan bread.
